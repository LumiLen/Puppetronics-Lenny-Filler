Puppetronics — Breathing Life into the Inanimate

My name is Lenny Filler, and I love sculpting human figures — or figures that question humanity in their own way. Each puppet begins with the joy of discovering the material, and often ends by surprising me, as if I’m only giving a small push before the magic continues on its own. I like combining mediums from different eras, exploring what separates the old from the new — not just in the material itself, but in how its meaning shifts within culture. My work often touches on the place of women in society, and on aesthetic and gendered perceptions of the body. I find comfort in puppets, and use both traditional methods and new tools, including AI, to experiment with animation, coding, and other techniques I never imagined using before.
