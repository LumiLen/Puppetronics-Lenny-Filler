Artist Statement

I build puppets and figurative objects to explore movement, fragility, and presence. My practice blends scenography with material research—cardboard, wood, wire—seeking tactile solutions that invite empathy and play.

Puppetronics is the meeting point between mechanism and character: how a joint, a string, or a counterweight becomes emotion in space.
