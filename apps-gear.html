Workflow (short)

  1. Photograph the puppet — 50–200 photos in even light.
  2. 3D from photos — Polycam / RealityCapture / Metashape / Meshroom.
  3. Clean the model in Blender.
  4. Rig (skeleton) — Mixamo or Rigify. Export .fbx.
  5. Motion capture — Rokoko Studio/Video.
  6. Retarget & animate — Rokoko / Mixamo rig.
  7. Render / export — Blender / FBX / GLB.

Apps I use

  • Blender
  • Mixamo
  • Rokoko
  • Polycam / Metashape / Meshroom
  • Substance Painter

Workflow (short)

  1. Photograph the puppet — 50–200 photos in even light.
  2. 3D from photos — upload to photogrammetry (Polycam / RealityCapture / Metashape / Meshroom).
  3. Clean the model in Blender: simplify, fix holes, UVs, bake textures.
  4. Rig (skeleton) — auto-rig with Mixamo or rig in Blender (Rigify). Export .fbx.
  5. Motion capture — record in Rokoko (Studio or Video). Export .bvh/.fbx.
  6. Retarget & animate — apply motion to the rig (Rokoko plugin / Mixamo rig).
  7. Render / export — Blender render or export FBX/GLB.

Apps I use

  • Blender — modeling, cleanup, rigging, rendering
  • Mixamo — quick auto-rig + test animations
  • Rokoko Studio / Rokoko Video — motion capture
  • Polycam / RealityCapture / Metashape / Meshroom — photogrammetry
  • Substance Painter — optional texturing

Tip: use diffuse light, avoid shiny surfaces, and overlap frames ~70%.
